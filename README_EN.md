**WinNet** - Subscription-based synchronization script
With this script, you can easily create multi-inbound accounts with all versions and forks of X-UI

---

## ‚≠êÔ∏è WinNet Synchronizer Script Features:

- Subscription-based synchronization in all inbounds every 30 seconds (synchronization according to the latest changes)
- Total and user traffic synchronization
- Traffic reduction/increase or reset synchronization
- Expiration date synchronization (decrease/increase/delete)
- Expiration date synchronization after the first connection
- Description synchronization (add/edit/delete)
- User limit synchronization (active/inactive/number)

### **Tunnel inbounds equalization**

- **Unification of tunnel & tun inbounds**
- Unification of up, down, total traffic of tunnel and tun inbounds in 3X-UI
- Unification of decrease/increase or reset of inbound traffic
- Unification of expiration date (decrease/increase/delete)
